About Me

I’ve been a hobbyist photographer for nearly 10 years, mostly taking photos of family and friends. I’ve always felt incredibly flattered when people ask me to take their photos, and even more so when I see these photos framed in their homes! It warms my little photographer heart! I’ve recently had formal photography training and this has not only elevated my skill level, but has me excited more than ever to get back out there.

My ultimate goal, is to volunteer for non-profit organizations and community outreach. I have in the past, taken portraits of foster children to assist in adoption and look forward to doing this again!

In the same context, I am currently available for Pay-What-You-Can Sessions. These are typically 20 minutes, with 5-7 edited images. Please reach out for more details!
