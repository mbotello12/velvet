About Me

  I had been an amateur photographer for nearly a decade, mostly taking photos of loved ones. After formal training under Jim Payne of Jim Payne Photography, my level of expertise was catapulted from a novice hobbyist to a published professional (2023). Photography is my love language, and if I ever ask you casually, or randomly (at an event), to take your photo, just know that is of the highest compliment I can offer someone.

  Moreover, I am an enthusiastic Volunteer Photographer for non-profit organizations and community outreach. I firmly believe in the power of photography to capture the essence of our humanity and to bring communities together. If there is any way I can use my skills to contribute to your cause, I am always ready and willing to collaborate!
